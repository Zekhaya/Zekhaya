Zekhaya Benard Shozi

(Pure Mathematician)

About Me


I am a lecturer under the New Generation of Academics Programme (nGAP) in the School of Natural and Applied Sciences at Sol Plaatje University, Kimberley. Before joining Sol Plaatje University, I was an MSc student at Stellenbosch University and I was based at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences. Currently, I am a PhD student in pure mathematics at the university of Johannesburg. I am conducting my PhD research under the supervision of Prof. Michael Henning, a research professor at the university of Johannesburg.

Research

My research interests are mainly on extremal graph theory, algebraic graph theory and group theory but I am generally interested in most areas of pure mathematics.
